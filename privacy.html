Gen Alpha Cricket Academy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 16 July 2026

This policy explains how Gen Alpha Cricket Academy handles personal information used for academy admissions, player management, attendance, fee payments, renewals, and related WhatsApp communication.

Information we collect

  • Player and guardian details, including names, age or date of birth, contact details, address, school, batch, and relevant coaching information.
  • Admission forms, conversations, corrections, and documents supplied by players, guardians, or authorised academy staff.
  • Payment and renewal information, including amount, date, payment method, transaction reference, and payment-proof images.
  • Attendance, coaching, jersey, communication, and account activity needed to operate the academy.

How we use information

We use this information to process admissions, maintain player records, schedule coaching, record attendance, reconcile fees and renewals, send service and payment reminders, respond to questions, prevent duplicate or incorrect records, and protect the academy's systems.

WhatsApp and automated processing

Messages sent to the academy's WhatsApp number may be processed through Meta's WhatsApp Business services. Conversations and attached images forwarded by authorised staff may also be analysed by an AI service to prepare a structured draft. A staff member must review and confirm the draft before an admission, renewal, or payment is recorded.

Service providers

We use service providers to host the academy application, store records, deliver WhatsApp messages, and assist with document and conversation extraction. These providers process information only to support the academy's operations and are subject to their own security and privacy obligations.

Retention and security

We retain information for as long as reasonably needed to operate the academy, maintain financial and player records, resolve disputes, and meet applicable obligations. We use access controls, staff confirmation, audit records, and technical safeguards designed to prevent unauthorised access or changes.

Your choices

Players and guardians may ask to review or correct their information, request deletion where appropriate, or opt out of non-essential WhatsApp communication. Some records may need to be retained for legitimate operational, financial, safety, or legal reasons.

Contact us

For privacy questions or requests, contact Gen Alpha Cricket Academy on WhatsApp or phone at +91 81439 60950.

We may update this policy when our services or processing practices change. The effective date above identifies the current version.
